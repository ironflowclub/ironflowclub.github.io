Privacy Policy

Iron Flow Club · Last updated: 9 June 2026

Iron Flow Club ("IFC", "we", "us") operates the IRONFLOW app at ironflowclub.github.io — a progressive web app for our running and badminton community in Sharjah, UAE. This policy explains what data we collect, why we use it, and your rights over it.

01

What Data We Collect

We only collect data that members voluntarily provide to us:

Player names — first name or display name used in tournament results.

Player photos — profile photos submitted by members for display on the Champions Archive.

Tournament results — match scores and team names from club events.

Usage analytics — anonymous data (pages visited, device type, country) collected via Google Analytics.

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, device type, and approximate location (city/country level). No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information. How Google uses this data →
02

How We Use Your Data

To display tournament results and champion history on the IFC website.

To recognise member achievements within the club community.

We do not sell, share, or pass your data to any third party.

03

Consent

We only publish a member's name or photo after receiving explicit consent from that member directly — via WhatsApp, a registration form, or written confirmation.

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us. We will remove your data from the website within 7 days of your request.

04

Data Storage

Tournament data is stored in Google Sheets and Supabase Storage, both of which operate under their own privacy and security standards. The website is hosted on GitHub Pages. No personal data is stored in website code or logs.

Analytics data is processed by Google Analytics (Google Ireland Limited) under their own Privacy Policy.

05

Your Rights

Under the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) and applicable regulations, you have the right to:

Know what data we hold about you.

Request correction of inaccurate data.

Request deletion of your data from the website.

Withdraw consent for your photo or name to be displayed.

06

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the club grows. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued participation in club events after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
