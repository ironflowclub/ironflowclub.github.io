IFC Training Program

Running

4-Week Cycle

Speed. Endurance. Tempo. Test day. Every four weeks, a new PB.

How It Works

Each week we meet and start with a short warm up followed by group runs in the following schedule below

The Cycle

Weekly Breakdown

Week 1

Speed Intervals

Building Pace

Format

10 min dynamic warmup, then 6 to 8 rounds of 1 min hard push followed by 2 min easy jog / walk recovery.

Week 2

Aerobic Endurance

Building Stamina

Format

10 min warmup, then 4 to 8 x 400m at a steady, conversational pace with a strict 90 sec walk recovery between loops. Finish with 10 min of core work.

Week 3

Tempo & Mechanics

Pace + Form

Format

10 min warmup, then 2 to 3km continuous at medium effort — no full conversation pace. Rest 3 min, then 4 x 100m relaxed strides to focus on form.

Week 4

The Long Run

Test Day

Format

A 5K+ time trial at Mamzar or Hera track — the measure of the cycle, and a shot at a new PB.
